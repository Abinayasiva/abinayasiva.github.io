OrgLimit Monitor

An independently developed and Salesforce-published AppExchange application with 100+ public installs, built to improve org-limit visibility and proactive admin alerts.

OrgLimit Monitor provides real-time alerts, proactive threshold monitoring, and on-demand insights for Salesforce org limits — a pervasive operational risk in enterprise Salesforce environments. Conceived, architected, and shipped as a solo AppExchange product.

Recognized as part of the 2026 FORUM Disruptive Technology Award at the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS), and covered by the International Business Times (UK).
